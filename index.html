Healthy Eating

Eating a balanced diet is key for a healthy lifestyle:

  • Include fruits and vegetables daily
  • Choose whole grains over processed grains
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Limit sugar and processed foods

Exercise

Regular physical activity keeps your body strong and fit:

  • 30 minutes of walking or jogging daily
  • Strength training 2–3 times a week
  • Stretching or yoga for flexibility

Sleep

Good sleep is essential for mental and physical health:

  • 7–9 hours of sleep per night
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Avoid screens before bedtime

Hobbies & Relaxation

Engaging in hobbies reduces stress and improves mood:

  • Reading books or listening to music
  • Painting, gardening, or crafts
  • Spending time with friends and family
